Local Experience is a platform for connecting you with locals in a new city who can show you their favorite places and share a bit of their culture. Avoid tourist traps as a traveler, find new places in your home town, and make new friends.

As a local with knowledge of your home town, make extra cash by spending time with travelers at your favorite restaurant, coffe shop, beach, bar, or hike and make new friends along the way. Set up a small profile with details about your interests and a brief description of where you like to go and what you like to do. Choose a rate to charge or offer your services for free.

This site is a prototype release and was created as a final project for the Stanford Startup Engineering class offered on Coursera. To get an idea of what this site offers, browse around by pressing the 'Experiences' button on the top or to the right. Sign up to offer a tour by pressing the 'Sign Up' button on this page. We aren't accepting payments at this time but will have the system up and running soon and will let you know as soon as this is possible.

FAQ

What does a donation offer me?
A huge thanks from the development team and future credit for use towards purchasing experiences from locals.
What benefit do I get from this service?
As a traveler, have a local show you around their neighborhood and find the best spots. As a local, earn a little extra cash by setting a rate for taking someone out. Both of you earn a new friend.
